Hi, my name is Berta Bernad. I’m a Photographer and Creative Director based in NYC.

I’ve done a few things I feel so proud of in my life. First, was studying journalism in The University Complutense of Madrid where I soon discovered my passion about photography and communication.

After a year of blogging about cool places and great people, I moved to London, where I worked in the Marketing department of Ralph Lauren and I improved my English a lot.

A year later (2012), I came back to the city I’ve grown up, Madrid. I’ve spent a year working for Aristocrazy launching their website. At the same time, I designed the Malmo bag and created fashion stories for vogue.es

This year I’m focusing on a new multi-brand website where I’ve put my heart on, the name is Curated by. I hope you like it! See you.
