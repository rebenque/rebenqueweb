A journey inspired by all the music that, in one way or another, makes us move. From the echoes of flamenco in Madrid and Cuban songs in New York, through the Caribbean and down the Andes, soaking up folklore all around us, we follow the rhythm to the southernmost tip of the Americas, and back home.

With our backpacks full of music and stories to tell in our own way.
